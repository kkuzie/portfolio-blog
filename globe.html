Imagined Enterprise

As with independent films whose works are produced outside the mainstream, Globe Records pioneered an outlet to work in tandem with independent musicians. The label aims to draw in an audience that appreciates the independent artists and in effect, help the artists succeed - on their terms.

This was a three-week personal project to design four distinct pages (desktop and mobile versions) for an up-and-coming record label.

challenge

Experience Through Feeling

Challenge was to create an immersive website experience where Globe Records' audience would 'feel' the music. Essential to the site design is the ability to showcase new artists and releases and serve as a central hub where artists, agents, and managers may submit demos, provide updates and offer memorabilia.

problems

solved

Scrutinizing design decisions

One version portrayed featured artists' photos on the "artists page" within a circular shape (mimicking records) with album covers on "releases page" in squares. Upon a trial run, the circles were judged to be too busy. Adjusting to square shapes on both pages provided more eye-pleasing clean lines with a fluid cohesion between the two.

In the building process one decision leads to another. Upon further scrutiny, it became apparent something was still lacking from the UI perspective. In this case, it inspired a valuable scrolling animation feature that focuses on each artist as they come into view.

All about the details

Original hover effect was left flat, leaving no impression on the visitor.

By adding micro-interaction with flair, the hover now added to the experience in a unique way. Reusing features in different ways created a connection to other areas of the site.

outcome

Stepping stone for expansion

Stage I provided solid stepping off points for further expansion of pages, details, and interactions.

finale

Hypnotic

Whereas the logo pulled from the idea of gold and platinum records, the site needed to cool down and hold attention almost hypnotically - like music in the background. The palette was pulled from concerts, spotlights, and colored lights blending together.

Streamlining Tasks

As the design developed, components within a design system were created in order to streamline repeatable features. This included interactive components in order to add enhancements/features that would further immerse the audience in the environment.

Commercial success

A previously produced video promotion of the company was the design inspiration. Adding it to the home page hero section gave the site life and a memorable first impression.
