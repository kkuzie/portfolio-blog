Hangin' atPo5 Pizza Lounge

local eatery

Po5 Pizza Lounge & Bar is a local downtown eatery that caters to families, tourists, and becomes the Gaslamp’s hot spot for night time entertainment. Offering a wide-range of menu items as well as pizza on-the-go.

Role: Director of Marketing, Graphic Designer

sketching it out

Playing off the restaurant’s official name, Pizza on 5th, I presented Po5 as a contemporary variation of the name and designed this logo. When unable to find a font that felt right to me, I was able to create a completely custom, original font to complement the logo. I incorporated the Po5 logo into a newly-designed menu — with added choices from my own recipes, largely inspired by my time living in Florence, Italy. With a mind geared towards completing a full package, my own personal vision for an elevated interior design ensued–but that is a “whole other story”!

Challenge:

• Non-memorable signage.
• No logo, lacking branding.
• Dated color palette.
• Business name not suitable for franchising future.

steps

Steps:

• Get to know what client envisioned, offered advice and ideas.
• Brainstorming concepts, names, colors.
• Sketching.
• Execution of logo, font, layouts.
• Presentation.
• Delivery.

solution

Solution:

• Full name change to Po5 Lounge and Bar - descriptive and modern.
• Logo designed for easy recognition - clean and crisp.
• Color palette created to allow business owner to convert from dated brown and yellow to a more contemporary blue and grey - a boost to the restaurant’s image and expanding clientele base.

result

Result:

• Rebranded business to facilitate future franchising potential. (“Pizza on 5th” wouldn’t align with a pizzeria on Main Street, now would it?)
• Upon name change to a hip, contemporary version (Po5), completed the transition with a personally designed, one-of-a-kind font to synchronize with corresponding menus and marketing tools.
