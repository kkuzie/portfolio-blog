Learnin' withETi

education website

San Diego Community Colleges District’s Employee Training Institute (ETi) provides education programs to students with the ability to earn degrees and skills certificates, as well as assist businesses, nonprofit organizations, and public entities with customized education and training for employees and/or clientele.

challenge

Team was tasked with the redesign of ETi's existing website, whose issues included an overload of text, disorganized content, and difficulty in locating and discerning the content and value of ETi's offerings.

Main goal was to provide an un-cluttered environment where potential clients would be enticed to explore the courses of study and training services via not only clear and concise descriptions but a pleasing visual experience paired with an intuitive, user-friendly navigational path.

original site

objective

The project was to design and code a minimum of four pages that avoided the frustration of muddling through the text-heavy current state of the site. Three main objectives were imperative:

• order and symmetry, as well as logical flow,
• have a properly defined, agreed upon navigation pattern for usability, and
• efficiency

brainstorming

The first door to unlock was the one into that got us into the collective mind of the site's audience. We investigated the pros and cons experienced by a variety of real-world website users and included astute consideration of ETi's expressed desires.

solution

A minimal, easily navigated interface slimmed down to four main pages solved the initial problem of being overwhelmed by text. Direct links to offered courses simplified the site further.

finale

The first four pages (Home, About, Students, Employers) were released in the incredibly short time span of just two months.

Since the initial phase outlined above, ETi has reached out to me in a freelance capacity for some continued maintenance and updates. I have added additional course pages, functionable email form, and created their new logo design that incorporates the existing "star" that is part of the San Diego Community College district logo (with whom ETi is affiliated). The analogy is that ETi is an "offshoot" of the college.
